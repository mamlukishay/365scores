Add yourself to the Predictions dashboard

This page builds your personal settings file so the dashboard can track your prediction leagues. Everything runs in your browser — nothing is sent anywhere.

1 · Copy your 365Scores token

A “token” is the key that lets the dashboard read your leagues. To get yours:

  1. Drag this button up to your browser’s bookmarks bar:  Grab 365Scores token
  2. Open 365scores.com in another tab and make sure you’re logged in.
  3. Click that new bookmark — it copies your token automatically.
  4. Come back to this page.
Can’t use the bookmark? Copy it by hand
  1. On 365scores.com, open DevTools (F12) → Network tab.
  2. Reload the page, then click any request to wcg-il.365scores.com.
  3. Under Request Headers, copy the authorization value (drop the leading Bearer ).

2 · Paste it and choose your leagues

Tick the leagues you want on your dashboard (all ticked by default):

    3 · Name your page

    This becomes your dashboard link, e.g. my-league → …/365scores/my-league

    4 · Save your settings

    Now submit it — the easiest way is the green button (GitHub opens with everything filled in; just confirm):

    No GitHub access? GitHub will offer to open a “pull request” — just confirm it, and the owner approves. Or use Copy/Download and send the file to the owner.

    
  
    

  
    
    One last thing: privately send your token to the dashboard owner so they can switch on automatic updates for you.
    Until they do — and the next update runs (within ~15 min) — your page will say “no data yet”.
    Your token is never saved into the project.
